ASAPer — Urgent Messenger

2012 - 2014

I co-founded ASAPer, an interactive messaging hub for home and corporate alarm systems that helps customers resolve alarms faster and reduces labor costs for central stations.

As the lead engineer, I designed and implemented a scalable API-based architecture and built client apps around the core API using Rails, Backbone and native iOS.

I led the initial market research and product design, pitched to potential customers at industry trade shows and grew our team to 6 full-time employees in just over a year.

Technologies used: Rails Backbone.js Marionette
